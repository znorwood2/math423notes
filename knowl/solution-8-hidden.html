The requested residue \(\Res\limits_{z=0}f(z)\) is the coefficient of \(z^4\) in the Taylor series expansion of \(\sinh(z)e^z\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \sinh(z)e^z = \left( z + \frac{z^3}{3!} + \cdots \right)\left( 1 + z + \frac{z^2}{2!} + \frac{z^3}{3!} + \cdots \right) = \cdots + (\frac{1}{4!} + \frac{1}{3!})z^4 + \cdots \end{equation*}
So \(\Res_{z=0}f(z) = \frac{1}{3!} + \frac{1}{4!} + \frac{5}{24}\text{.}\)
