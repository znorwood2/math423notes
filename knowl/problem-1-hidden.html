Prove that the following identities hold for all \(z,w\in\C\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \Re(z) = \displaystyle\frac{z+\bar z}{2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \Im(z) = \displaystyle\frac{z - \bar z}{2i}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \bar{z+w} = \bar z + \bar w\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \bar{zw} = \bar z \cdot \bar w\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \abs{z}^2 = z\cdot \bar z\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \abs{zw} = \abs z \cdot \abs w\)
  7. \(\displaystyle \abs{z+w}^2 = \abs{z}^2 + \abs{w}^2 + 2\Re(z\bar w)\)
