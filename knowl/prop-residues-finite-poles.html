Proposition 6.1.17. Residues at finite poles.

If \(f\) has a pole of order \(k\) at \(a\) then \(g(z) = (z-a)^k f(z)\) is analytic at \(z=a\) and
\begin{equation*} \Res_{z=a}f(z) = c_{k-1} = \frac{g^{(k-1)}(a)}{(k-1)!}, \end{equation*}
where \(c_{k-1}\) is the coefficient of \((z-a)^{k-1}\) in the Taylor seris for \(g\text{.}\)
