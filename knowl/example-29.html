Example 4.1.5.

Compute \(\int_{OABO} f(z)\,dz\) over the triangular curve through the points \(O = 0\text{,}\) \(A = i\text{,}\) and\(B = 1+i\text{,}\) where \(f(x+iy) = (y-x) -3ix^2\text{.}\)
We parametrize the three sides of the triangle and integrate along each piece separately; then we add the results together.
Along \(OA\) you should get \(\frac{i}{2}\text{.}\) Along \(AB\) you should get \(\frac12 - i\text{.}\) Along \(BO\) you should get \(1-i\text{.}\) The total is \(\frac{i - 1}{2}\text{.}\)
