Theorem 4.5.5. The Cauchy Integral Formula for Derivatives.

Suppose that \(\gamma\) is a simple closed curve and \(f\) is analytic that includes \(\gamma\) and its interior. Assume that \(\gamma\) is counterclockwise-oriented. Then for all \(z\) inside \(\gamma\text{,}\) the \(n\)th derivative \(f^{(n)}(z)\) exists and satisfies the following formula.
\begin{equation*} f^{(n)}(z) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i} \oint_\gamma \frac{f(w)}{(w - z)^{n+1}} \, dw \end{equation*}

Proof.
