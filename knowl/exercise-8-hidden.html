Let \(\bar\bF\) be the vector field \(\angs{u,-v}\) associated to the conjugate of \(f = u + iv\text{.}\) Prove that
\begin{equation*} \int_\gamma f(z)\,dz = \operatorname{Work}(\bar\bF,\gamma) + i\operatorname{Flux}(\bar\bF,\gamma), \end{equation*}
where
\begin{align*} \operatorname{Work}(\bG,\gamma) \amp = \int_\gamma (\bG \cdot \bT) \, ds \quad \text{and}\\ \operatorname{Flux}(\bG,\gamma) \amp = \int_\gamma (\bG \cdot \bN) \, ds \end{align*}
