Example 4.5.3.

Compute \(\int_\gamma \frac{e^{z^2}}{z-2}\,dz\text{,}\) where \(\gamma\) parametrizes a circle around \(2\text{.}\)
Notice that \(f(z) = e^{z^2}\) is entire, so the Cauchy Integral Formula gives
\begin{equation*} e^4 = f(2) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \int_{\gamma} \frac{e^{z^2}}{z-2}\,dz, \end{equation*}
which we solve to see that the integral is \(2\pi i e^4\text{.}\)
