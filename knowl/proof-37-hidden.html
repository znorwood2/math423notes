If the two series converge to \(f\) on \(B_r(a)\text{,}\) then (exercise) they are analytic there. (You can differentiate term by term.) So we must have \(f(a) = c_0 = d_0\text{,}\) \(f'(a) = c_1 = d_1\text{,}\) \(f''(a) = 2c_2 = 2d_2\text{,}\) etc. (Proceed by induction.)
