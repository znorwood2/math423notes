Item b.

Suppose that \(\gamma\colon[a,b]\to\C\) is a contour and that \(c\in [a,b]\text{.}\) Let \(\gamma_{\text{start}}\) be the restriction of \(\gamma\) to \([a,c]\) and let \(\gamma_{\text{end}}\) be the restriction of \(\gamma\) to \([c,b]\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \int_\gamma f(z)\,dz = \int_{\gamma_{\text{start}}} f(z)\,dz + \int_{\gamma_{\text{end}}} f(z)\,dz \end{equation*}
